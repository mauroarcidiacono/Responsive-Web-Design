Introduction

ArtSnares© was founded in 1951 in the house of Mark Timmerman, an immigrant from the Netherlands. He worked repairing and renewing old furniture and while he was doing his job, he used to listen to his favorite jazz songs. As a hobby, Mr. Timmerman decided to build a snare because he was a big fan of percussionists. Mark dedicated several years until he perfected a patented construction technique that we carry as manufacturers today. His legacy lives in each snare and we only sell unique pieces that have been used by some of the most famous jazz players. ArtSnares© sound is crafted by expert hands and it is characterized by a dynamic and smooth sound, that can be adapted for multiple styles.
